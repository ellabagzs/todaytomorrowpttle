CAUSES & SOLUTIONS
WHAT CAUSES AIR POLLUTION
1. burning of biodegradable waste
2. smoking
3. smoke from public vehicles
4. deforestation
5. natural forest wildfires
SOLUTIONS FOR AIR POLLUTION
1. save electricity, turn off lights when not in use
2. practice the 3r's: reduce, reuse, recycle
3. avoid usage of plastic materials such as plastic bags, straws, and bottles
4. reduction of the rate of forest fires and smoking
5. avoid the usage of firecrackers during special occasions
